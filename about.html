Introduction

Empowering Generations Since 2007

Dr. K.V. Subba Reddy Institute of Technology (KVSRIT), established in 2007, is a premier institution located in Kurnool. Promoted by Vaibhav Educational Society (VES), the college has grown into a prestigious center for engineering and management education.

With accreditation from NAAC A+ Grade and affiliation to JNTUA, Ananthapuramu, KVSRIT stands as a testament to quality education and institutional excellence.
